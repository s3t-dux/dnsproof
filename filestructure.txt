NOTE: 
dnsproof is the directory that contains the internal FastAPI on the nameserver VM. It communicates with the app backend.
app is the directory that builds the frontend app. routes, utils, config.py and main.py are the files for the app backend.
bootstrap_vm_portabl.sh is the shell script that provisions nameservers.
